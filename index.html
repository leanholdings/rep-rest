When you email

Including these helps us troubleshoot quickly:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • Rep&Rest app version
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • A screenshot (if relevant)

Common questions

  • How do I set reps and rest? Adjust the Reps and Rest perios using the spinners or manually enter your value in seconds in the input fields below the spinners, then start the timer.
  • Sound/vibration not working? Check the phone’s mute switch / Focus modes and in-app sound settings.
  • Feature request? Email us—tell us your ideal workflow.

Rep&Rest is designed to be simple and reliable. If something feels off, let us know.
